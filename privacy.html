Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-01-01  |  FandorinGames Content Publisher

Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to the FandorinGames Content Publisher Tool, operated by the FandorinGames brand. Contact: fandorinpl@gmail.com

Data We Access

This Tool accesses the following data via the TikTok API, solely for publishing purposes:

user.info.basic video.publish

No data is accessed from any account other than the FandorinGames TikTok account owned by the operator.

Data Storage

  • OAuth access tokens are stored locally on the operator's machine only
  • No user data is transmitted to third-party servers
  • Tokens are not logged, shared, or uploaded to any external service
  • Video files processed by the Tool remain on the operator's local infrastructure

Third-Party Services

The Tool interacts with the following external services:

  • TikTok API — for video publishing. Subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic Claude API — for content generation. No personal data is transmitted.
  • Public RSS feeds and Steam API — read-only, no personal data involved.

No Third-Party Data Collection

This Tool does not collect, process, or store personal data belonging to any third party, viewer, or TikTok user. It operates solely on content produced by and for the FandorinGames operator.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as the Tool evolves. The effective date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
